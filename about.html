20 Saltwater Fishing Facts

  • 1. The Western Cape has both Atlantic and Indian Ocean coastlines.
  • 2. False Bay is a top spot for Kob, Shad, and Yellowtail.
  • 3. Cape Point offers deep-sea fishing for Tuna and Snoek.
  • 4. Kalk Bay is famous for pier fishing and fresh bait.
  • 5. Galjoen is South Africa’s national fish—often caught near rocky shores.
  • 6. The best surf fishing happens during rising tides.
  • 7. Sardines and chokka are top bait choices.
  • 8. West Coast towns like Paternoster and Langebaan are great for boat fishing.
  • 9. Snoek runs peak in winter months.
  • 10. Estuaries like Knysna and Witsand are ideal for Grunter and Steenbras.
  • 11. Fishing licenses are required for all saltwater fishing.
  • 12. Bag and size limits protect fish populations—check local rules.
  • 13. Many species are seasonal—plan your trips accordingly.
  • 14. Rock and surf fishing is popular along the Cape Peninsula.
  • 15. Weather and swell conditions affect catch success.
  • 16. Local tackle shops offer region-specific gear and advice.
  • 17. Conservation areas may restrict fishing—always check signage.
  • 18. Night fishing can be productive for Kob and Elf.
  • 19. Many anglers use drone casting to reach deeper waters.
  • 20. Western Cape fishing is a mix of tradition, sport, and sustainability.
